Tea Recipe
This is a web page for someone who wants to know how to make tea. It contains a list of ingredients and an ordered list of steps in making tea.

    Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Tea/Teabag

    Method:

  1. Boil water in kettle.
  2. Place teabag in cup.
  3. Leave teabag steeping in water for 3 minutes then remove.
  4. Add milk, sugar, etc...
  5. Stir and enjoy!
