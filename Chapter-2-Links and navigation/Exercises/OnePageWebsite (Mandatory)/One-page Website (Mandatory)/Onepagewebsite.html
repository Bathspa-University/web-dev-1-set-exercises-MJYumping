DIE HARD
Plot Characters Cast and Creative Team Reviews

PLOT

The plot of Die Hard revolves around a New York City cop named John McClane, who travels to Los Angeles to reconcile with his estranged wife, Holly Genero. As he connects back with his wife, German terrorists would sieze control of the Nakatomi Plaza, the high-rise building owned by the Nakatomi Corporation of where she works. After narrowly evading capture, John McClane attempts to subdue and save the civilians whilst navigating the high-rise using air vents, elevator shafts, and empty floors.

CHARACTERS

John McClane

John McClane is the protagonist of the movie franchise Die Hard. An Irish-American detective and lieutenant of the NYPD, he is a married man with 2 kids. Throughout the movie franchise, he is characterized as "the wrong guy, at the wrong place, in the wrong time" often finding that chaos and crime finds itself in the same place that John McClane goes.

Holly Gennaro McClane

Holly Gennaro McClane is the tritagonist of the first two movies of the movie franchise Die Hard. The wife of John McClane, Holly Gennaro's character was an employee of the Nakatomi Corporation and was taken hostage by the German terrorists. She plays a supportive role to John McClane, often taking the right opportunity at the right time.

Hans Gruber

Hans Gruber is the main protagonist of Die Hard and is the leader of the German terrorists that took control of the Nakatomi Corporation Plaza. A measured and quick-witted man, he would meticulously plan a heist for the Nakatomi Corporation's bearer bonds disguised as an act of terrorism.

Sgt. Al Powell

Sgt. Al Powell is the deuteragonist of the film Die Hard. Al Powell is an LAPD cop that guides John McClane throughout the events of the movie. Serving as the lone level headed person John McClane would have communications with from the outside, he makes a heroic moment by neutralizing the last remaining villain in the movie.

Cast and Creative Team

Bruce Willis

Bruce Willis is an American actor known for works such as The Last Boy Scout, Pulp Fiction, Armageddon, and Die Hard. Born in West Germany, he would be a recipient of multiple awards such as a Golden Globe Awards, and a star on the hollywood walk of fame.

Bonnie Bedelia

Bonnie Bedelia is an American actor known for works such as The Gypsy Moths, Needful Things, and Die Hard. Born in New York, she would earn two Emmy Award nominations.

Alan Rickman

Alan Rickman was an English actor and director known for playing Severus Snape in the Harry Potter series, and Hans Gruber in Die Hard. Born in London, England, Alan Rickman is a recipient of multiple BAFTA Awards.

Reginald ValJohnson

Reginald ValJohnson is an American actor known for playing police officer characters, such as Carl Winslow, and LAOPD Sargeant Al Powell. He Has obtained a Bachelor of Fine Arts in theater from New York University.
